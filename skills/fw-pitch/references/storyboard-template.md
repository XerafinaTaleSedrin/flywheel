## Field Notes

* **Insight** must be tied to the positioning canvas's market frame of reference. A free-floating insight that doesn't connect to the canvas is suspect.

* **Old Game** alternatives must match the competitive alternatives in the canvas. If an alternative shows up here that isn't in the canvas, either update the canvas or drop the alternative.

* **New Game criteria** should be 3-5. Fewer than 3 means the insight isn't rich enough; more than 5 means the buyer won't remember them.

* **Perfect Solution** must be described abstractly. If it sounds exactly like your product, you're skipping ahead to Step 5.

* **Differentiated Value** table must reference attributes from the canvas. The drift detector enforces this.

* **Proof gaps** are not failures — they're roadmap signals. Surface them explicitly.

* **The Ask** must be a single specific next step, not a menu of options.

* **Spoken Arc** is the test: if you can't say it out loud in one breath, the storyboard is too complicated.
